What I need from you

Before we get started

The more you can pull together before we begin, the faster and smoother the build. Don't worry if some of this isn't ready — we can work through it together.

01

Business details

Your business name, address, phone number, email, ABN, and trading hours. The basics that appear across the site.

02

Logo files or we discuss options

Your logo in PNG or SVG format, ideally with a transparent background. If you don't have one, let me know and we'll work something out.

03

Brand colours & fonts if you have them

Any existing colour palette or typography you use. If you don't have these, I'll develop something that suits your business.

04

Photos

Images of your business, products, team, or premises. Good photos make a huge difference. Phone shots are fine — I can work with what you have.

05

Page content or I can help

The text for each page — what you do, your story, your services. If writing isn't your thing, I can draft copy based on our briefing conversation.

06

Domain & hosting access if existing

Login details for your domain registrar and hosting account if you already have them. If you're starting fresh, I'll help you set these up.
