What I need from you

Before we get started

The more you can pull together before we begin, the faster and smoother the build.

01

Business details

Full business name, address, phone, email, ABN, trading hours, and all existing online presence links.

02

Logo & brand files or we discuss

Logo in PNG or SVG, any existing brand guidelines, colour palette, and font preferences.

03

Professional photos

High quality images of your business, food, team, venue, or products. The more the better for a 20-page site.

04

Full site content or I can help

Text for every page. Given the scope, I strongly recommend discussing copywriting support as part of your quote.

Booking system details

Your preferred booking platform, service types, capacity, pricing, and availability rules.

Admin access to all existing Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn accounts for setup and optimisation.

Google account access

Access to your Google account for Business Profile setup and Search Console configuration.

Domain & hosting if existing

Login details for domain registrar and hosting.

Competitor & inspiration sites optional

Any websites you admire or competitors you're aware of.
