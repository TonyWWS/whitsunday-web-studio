How I got here

Early career

Ten years in professional kitchens

I spent a decade working in professional kitchens — learning how hospitality businesses actually operate from the inside. The pressure of service, the importance of presentation, the reality of running a business where margins are tight and every customer interaction matters.

Retail

Into hospitality retail

Moving into hospitality retail gave me a different perspective — the customer-facing side, stock management, staff, and the constant challenge of standing out in a competitive local market. I watched businesses succeed and fail, and I started to notice that the ones who struggled online struggled everywhere.

Whitsundays

Seven years in the region

Moving to Airlie Beach gave me an even clearer picture. The Whitsundays is a region full of brilliant small businesses — cafés, tour operators, accommodation providers — many of whom are being underserved by digital agencies that don't understand their seasonal business models, their reliance on tourism, or the specific challenges of operating in a regional area.

Now

Whitsunday Web Studio

I retrained in IT, completed my Certificate III, and built Whitsunday Web Studio with one goal: to give local businesses access to quality digital work from someone who actually understands their industry. Every site I build draws on 20 years of knowing what hospitality and tourism businesses need — not just what looks good on screen.
