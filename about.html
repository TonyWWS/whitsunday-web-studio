How I got here

Early career

Ten years in professional kitchens

A decade in professional kitchens teaches you things no business course covers. Tight margins mean every decision counts, consistent results depend on solid procedures, and the consumer is more fickle than most business owners want to admit. I was in the middle of that every day.

Retail

Into retail

Retail taught me the value of marketing and the power of exclusivity. A well-positioned product with a strong presence will outsell a better product with a weak one almost every time. It is even more important now, with so many buying decisions made without the customer casting a shadow on your door.

Whitsundays

Nearly a decade in the region

The Whitsundays has so many brilliant small businesses, though I have noticed more than a few are getting by with a Google Business Profile and a meagre social media presence. A clean, functional, well-positioned website should not be a luxury. Not only that, it is usually synonymous with a thriving business.

Now

Whitsunday Web Studio

I retrained through TAFE Queensland, got my Certificate III in IT, and launched Whitsunday Web Studio. I genuinely believe that local businesses deserve better digital representation. I also believe that providing it locally strengthens our community while proudly showcasing the Whitsundays to the world.
