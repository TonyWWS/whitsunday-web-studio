Local search

Google Business Profile

When someone searches "café Airlie Beach" or "tour operator Whitsundays", the businesses that appear in the map pack — the three listings that show up before the search results — get the majority of the clicks and calls.

A properly set up and optimised Google Business Profile is one of the most powerful free tools available to local businesses. Most aren't using it to its full potential, which means the ones that do stand out immediately.

I set it up correctly, get it verified, optimise it with the right keywords and categories, and make sure your information is complete, consistent, and working in your favour every time someone searches locally.
